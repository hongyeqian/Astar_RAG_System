# Summary

Martial arts legend **Jackie Chan** discussed his career and the physical toll of his work in an interview with Steve Harvey. He expressed great honor in receiving the **Honorary Oscar** (the first Chinese actor to do so) but detailed his extensive list of **broken bones** and injuries over the years. Chan highlighted his frustration with Hollywood's persistent **typecasting** in **"action comedy"** roles (e.g., police/CIA), stating his ambition to pursue more **serious** and **romantic** genres, such as films involving slow motion and kissing. He promoted his new film, **The Foreigner**, which allows him to play a more dramatic, older character. This information is relevant for topics covering film industry typecasting, martial arts careers, and actor ambitions.
