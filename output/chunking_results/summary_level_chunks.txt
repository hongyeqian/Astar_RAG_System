# Summary

In an interview with Cleo Abram, Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang details the fundamental computing shift his company led, starting with the insight into parallel processing (GPU) for video games, which evolved with the creation of the CUDA platform. Huang outlines his vision for the future, where "everything that moves will be robotic"; he explains that these physical AI systems will be trained in digital twin simulators like Omniverse (for physics) and Cosmos (a "world foundation model") to gain physical common sense.
